Birth : 2 NOV 1795, Mecklenburg Co., North Carolina
Death : 15 JUN 1849, Nashville, Tennessee
Burial : Nashville, Tennessee

Father : Samuel Polk - b. 1772 d. 1827

Mother : Jane Knox - b. 1776 d. 1852

Spouse : Sarah Childress - b. 4 SEP 1803, Nr Murfreesboro, Tennessee d. 14 AUG 1891, Nashville, Tennessee

Married 1 JAN 1824, Murfreesboro, Rutherford Co., Tennessee

Note: Was elected over Henry Clay by a popular vote of 1,338,464 to 1,300,097 and an
electoral vote of 170 to 105. George M. Dallas served as his vice-president.
Occasionally called "Little Hickory". During his presidency the USA grew by
800,000 square miles, including California, New Mexico, Arizona and Texas;
the boundaries between Canada and the USA were set where they are today. Polk
did not seek a second term and, worn out from his hard work, died three months
after leaving office.

Occupation:: US President No. 11
